INDIABULLS SKY - LUXURY HOMES IN MUMBAI

Indiabulls Sky is situated at the heart of the Golden Mile - a glorious stretch developed and maintained by Indiabulls Real Estate, which is a perfect harmonisation of the city’s legendary residential; and commercial landmarks.
The unhindered view of the pristine Arabian Sea, the wondrous Worli Sea Link, the lush green Mahalaxmi Race Course and the gorgeous skyline of Mumbai make every day a dream at Indiabulls Sky. Life at Indiabulls Sky is elevated as from its 16th floor, begins the 96 exclusive full height glazed luxury apartments with majestic terraces which are now ready-for-fit-outs.
Be it the double-heighted terraces, the infinity pool or the grand quadruple-heighted lobby, each part of this luxury residential project adds to its elegance making it a prime choice for luxury homes in Lower Parel. The sophisticated world-class turnkey interiors in each apartment are designed by the most revered professionals. Spread across various levels, a host of exclusive amenities ranging from fitness centres to swimming pools to theatres make living, playing and socialising an experience to elevate your senses and make you feel like a dream come true in your own luxury flat in lower parel.
Indiabulls sky has received its OC and has ready-to-move-in flats available as well!
